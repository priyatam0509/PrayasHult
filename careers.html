Be a part of something great

We are 4000+ people strong. Ninjacart is the place for innovators, problem-solvers and executionists. If you're looking for a job that presents real-world challenges and lets you explore your potential, the infectious energy here will make you create wonders that exceed your own expectations.

Why we do it

For decades, the traditional Fresh Produce Supply Chain was highly inefficient with zero food security, until a few brave men decided to tackle the age-old problem and built a 100% traceable supply chain.

We, at Ninjacart, intend to improve the lives of producers, business owners, and consumers in a meaningful manner while solving one of the hardest supply chain problems our country has to offer with technology. Know more >
